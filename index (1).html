Key Insights

Biggest Leak

The Visitor → Lead step loses ~95% of traffic — by far the largest drop of any stage. Even a small lift here compounds through every downstream stage.

Best Lead Quality

Referral and Email convert leads to customers 2–4x better than paid channels, despite bringing in far less volume.

Worst Spend Efficiency

Content Syndication produced zero customers this year while costing the most per lead — the clearest candidate for budget reallocation.
